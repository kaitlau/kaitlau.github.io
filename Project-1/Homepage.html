BRAISED PORK BELLY

Shanghai-Style Braised Pork Belly (hong shao rou, 红烧肉), or“red cooked pork,”is a very famous dish in China. Everyone knows it, and there are many versions and twists based on the original. Some of the more well-known variations include the addition of squid, hard boiled eggs, and tofu knots.
